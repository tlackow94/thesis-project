Literature Review

Environmental Impact of Solar and Wind Energy — A Review

Abu Hamed, T., & Alshare, A. · 2022 · Journal of Sustainable Development of Energy, Water and Environment Systems

An investigation of the environmental impacts of solar and wind energy, including impacts on biodiversity, noise, glare, and others.

Proposed design of wind turbine for highway energy harvesting

Azhar, A. D. E., & Rosly, N. · 2025 · Proceedings of Applied & Advanced Technologies (PAAT).

A comparison of the Darrieus and Savonius wind turbines against previous designs, focusing on parameters such as wind, flow, fluid density, and other factors.

Opportunities for value extraction projects in highway rights-of-way and development of feasibility assessment tool

Bayraktar, M. E., Arif, F., Ponder, D., Prozzi, J., & Mahmoud, N. V. · 2016 · Journal of Construction Engineering and Management

An overview of how highway value extraction techniques such as solar PV cells, and agriculture. Also includes a screening tool to determine feasibility.

Optimal location of solar photovoltaic plants using geographic information systems and multi-criteria analysis

de Luis-Ruiz, J. M., Salas-Menocal, B. R., Pereda-García, R., Pérez-Álvarez, R., Sedano- Cibrián, J., & Ruiz-Fernández, C. · 2024 · Sustainability

An examination of using MCA to determine siting for solar cells. Includes various criteria for assessing suitability.
